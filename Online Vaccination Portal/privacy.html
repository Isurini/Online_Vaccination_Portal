  • Introduction

    • Welcome to CHILED SHEILD! We at CHILED SHEILD value your privacy and your personal information. This Privacy Policy will explain how we collect, use, and protect data when you access our online vaccination portal. We operate in adherence with all applicable privacy laws and regulations to responsibly handle your information. We earn your trust through being in care of security and confidentiality regarding your personal data.

  • Information We Collect

    1. Personal Information
      • Name
      • Date of Birth
      • Email Address
      • Phone Number
      • Address
      • Health Information (e.g., vaccination history)
    2. Non-Personal Information
      • Browser type and version
      • IP address
      • Pages visited
      • Time and date of visits
      • Referring website

  • How We Use Your Information

    • The information we collect from you is used for the following purposes in general:

    • Providing and maintaining our Service.
    • Notifying you about changes to our Service.
    • Allowing you to participate in interactive features of our Service when you choose to do so.
    • Providing customer support.
    • For analytics, or valuable information that may provide an insight enabling us to improve our.
    • To track usage of the Service.
    • To detect, prevent and address technical issues.

  • Disclosure of Your Information

    • We may disclose your information in the following circumstances:

    • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, contractors, or agents who perform services for or on behalf of us.
    • For Legal Reasons: If the law so permits, we shall disclose your information, based on a request by public authorities.
    • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal data is transferred
    • With Affiliates: We may share your information with our affiliates, in which case we will require those affiliates to honor this Privacy Policy.
    • With Your Consent: We may disclose your personal information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

  • Security of your Information

    • We are concerned about the security of your personal information. Hence, we put in place reasonable security measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, use, change, and disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data; thus, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your personal information.

  • Your Rights

    • we respect your privacy and want you to know your rights regarding your personal information :

    • Access: A right to copies of your personal data.
    • Correction of Inaccurate or Incomplete Information: A right to have us correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
    • Erasure: A right to request that we delete your personal data under certain conditions.
    • Right to object: You have a right to object to our processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    • We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

  • Third-Party Links

    • Our Service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy and terms of service of every site you visit.

  • Children's Privacy

    • Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us. If we learn that we have collected personal data from children under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to delete that information from.

  • Changes to This Privacy Policy

    • We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the revised policy on this page and update the effective date. We recommend that you periodically review this Privacy Policy for any changes.

  • How to Contact Us

    • Please contact Support if you have any question or concerns not already addressed in this Privacy Policy.
